# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

[中文](#中文) | [English](#english)

---

## 中文

### 1. 文档目的

本文件用于帮助 AntiGEO 社区保持公开、透明、克制、可持续的协作环境。项目讨论可能涉及品牌、组织、域名、证据、申诉与复审，但社区互动仍应保持基本的尊重与边界。

### 2. 我们鼓励的行为

我们鼓励以下行为：

- 以证据和上下文为中心讨论
- 尊重不同意见与复审机制
- 指出问题时尽量具体、清楚、可复核
- 帮助改进文档、术语、结构和记录准确性
- 在争议问题上保持克制与耐心
- 愿意接受自己的观点被补充、修正或推翻

### 3. 我们不接受的行为

我们不接受以下行为：

- 人身攻击
- 骚扰
- 恶意挑衅
- 侮辱性语言
- 竞争性抹黑
- 把项目当作品牌攻击或舆论动员工具
- 明知证据不足仍反复进行情绪化指控
- 以申诉、提案、评论等形式进行骚扰式滥用

### 4. 关于争议性讨论的特别说明

AntiGEO 的讨论对象可能具有争议性，但讨论本身必须围绕证据、定义、流程和记录进行。我们不鼓励情绪化、标签化或定罪式表达，也不应因为对象有争议就降低证据标准或行为标准。

### 5. 关于协作与修正

社区成员可以指出错误、补充上下文、提交反证。文档、提案、证据和状态都可能被修正。被纠正不是攻击，纠正他人也应尽量保持克制、具体和清晰。纠错、复审和补充上下文本身就是协作的一部分。

### 6. 早期项目说明

AntiGEO 仍处于早期阶段，行为准则未来可能通过社区公开修订，但整体目标始终是保持一个透明、克制、可协作的社区环境。

## English

### 1. Purpose of This Document

This document is intended to help the AntiGEO community maintain an open, transparent, restrained, and sustainable collaborative environment. Project discussions may involve brands, organizations, domains, evidence, appeals, and re-review, but community interaction should still remain within basic standards of respect and restraint.

### 2. Behavior We Encourage

We encourage the following:

- Evidence and context centered discussion
- Respectful disagreement and respect for re-review mechanisms
- Raising issues in ways that are specific, clear, and verifiable
- Helping improve documentation, terminology, structure, and record accuracy
- Staying restrained and patient in disputed matters
- Being willing to have one's own view supplemented, corrected, or overturned

### 3. Behavior We Do Not Accept

We do not accept the following:

- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Malicious provocation
- Inflammatory or demeaning language
- Competitive smear behavior
- Using the project as a tool for brand targeting or public-opinion mobilization
- Repeated emotional accusations despite clearly insufficient evidence
- Harassing abuse through appeals, proposals, comments, or similar channels

### 4. A Note on Disputed Topics

AntiGEO may discuss disputed or controversial subjects, but discussions should remain focused on evidence, definitions, process, and records. Emotional framing, labeling, or conviction-style language is discouraged. A controversial subject does not justify lowering either the evidence standard or the behavior standard.

### 5. Collaboration and Correction

Community members may point out errors, add missing context, or submit counter-evidence. Documentation, proposals, evidence, and statuses may all be corrected. Being corrected is not an attack, and correcting others should also be done with restraint, clarity, and specificity. Correction is part of collaboration.

### 6. Early-Stage Project Note

AntiGEO is still at an early stage, and this code of conduct may be revised publicly in the future. Its overall purpose remains the same: to maintain a transparent, restrained, and collaborative community environment.
